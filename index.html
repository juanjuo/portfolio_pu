Which occurs right in the middle of the revolution for Venezuelan independence from the Spanish monarchy. The piece is constituted by 7 movements, each of which is linked to one important aspect of the revolution, and/or one important battle of the campaign, each movement explores both the underlying structure and happenings of the battle, and the socio-political context of that event. Text-to-Speech - This work is a 30 minutes multimedia performance I composed for myself as part of the senior recital requirement for my undergraduate degree. In the work I explore a lot of themes that are personal to my life and my experience as a student in the United States, framed through the context of a letter I wrote to my father. This work also makes use of some of the technology I developed for my senior thesis for my second degree in Computer Science. It showcases some of the work I have been doing in the last year and the kind of art I am interested in doing going forward in my creative career. This piece feels like a draft to me. I don't intend it to be played by anybody else, or to be performed ever again. Though, I find it worth sharing, since it is a combination of raw materials deeply rooted in my experience as a performer/composer, as a student at Bard College, and emotional self. Please listen to the first 5 minutes as an excerpt for my application.
